# Job Seeker App

Job Seeker App to find the best suitable job for you !

# App Features

> Job Search Categories

> Build your Profile 

> Recommended Job

> Track your applications

> AR is integrated to get city wise statistics of job data

> Featured / Treding Jobs in your domain
